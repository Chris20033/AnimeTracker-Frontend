# Make Interfaces Feel Better

Use this skill for tactile polish and perceived quality improvements.

## Focus Areas

- Typography scale, weight, line-height, and optical alignment.
- Surface layering with subtle borders and shadows.
- Hover, active, disabled, loading, and focus states.
- Comfortable hit areas for mouse and touch.
- Small transitions that clarify interaction without distracting.
- Empty and success states that feel deliberate.

## Constraints

- Keep motion compositor-friendly.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Do not overuse gradients, blur, or glow.
- Preserve existing product tone unless the user asks for a redesign.
